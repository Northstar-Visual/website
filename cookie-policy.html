This policy explains the cookies used on northstarvisual.co.uk and how to control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

Cookies We Use

Name / Provider Type Purpose Duration
Strictly Necessary Essential Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled. Session
Google Analytics (_ga, _gid) Analytics Measures traffic and usage to help us improve the site. Only set with your consent. Up to 2 years
Google Tag Manager (_gtm) Analytics Manages the loading of analytics and tracking tags. Only active if GTM is configured. Session

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies via your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Allow cookies only from specific websites

Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

For more information on managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org or the ICO's cookie guidance.
